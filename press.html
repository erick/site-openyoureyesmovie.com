"Open Your Eyes" Nominated for a Grand OFF Award 2009

"Open Your Eyes" is 1 of the 5 nominated films selected out of more than 700 shorts for a Grand OFF Award 2009 in the category of Best Directing. The nominees include "Lazarus Taxon" (Spain), "After Tomorrow" (UK), "The Door" (Ireland), "In Nomine Patris" (France), and "Open Your Eyes" (USA). The 5 nominated films have been sent to the members of the Grand OFF Jury Chapter from all over the world. The fate of the nominations, which lies in the hands of 55 Grand OFF Jury Members from 24 countries, will be decided on the 21st of November, during the Grand OFF Awards Gala in Warsaw.
