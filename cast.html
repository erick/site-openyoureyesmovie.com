THE CAST

Traci Dinwiddie
"Julia"




Traci Dinwiddie is one of the unique female guest stars recurring as badass and blind psychic, Pamela Barnes, on CW's "Supernatural". She has also guest starred on 90210 as the British charlatan, Madame Flanagan as well as shared the screen with Demi Moore as her psychotic kidnapper, Sarah Leaves, in the feature film "Mr. Brooks". As a seasoned stage actress, she's tackled a diverse range of roles such as Patsy Cline, Titania, Lady M, and Mrs. Forrest. A dynamic force on and off screen, she fills her days with West African drumming, Anusara Yoga, and myriad forms of community improv theater. Her motto in life is Rumi's poetry... "Let the beauty we love be what we do".
