THE TEAM

Alisa Wiegers
-Producer




Alisa's background in the creative arts is varied and diverse. She has worked professionally as an actress in film, television, theatre and commercials in both her native Canada and the United States. Her greatest satisfaction has always been working on independent film and theatre projects. She played the lead roles in HAVE MERCY and PARANOID, independent films which have aired on Showcase, Bravo, TMN and at the Montreal Film Festival.

Also a writer, Alisa co-wrote two plays, HIDE AND SEEK and DR. FEELGOOD, both of which were produced at the Toronto Summerworks Festival. As a writer/director she was shortlisted for both the prestigious National Screen Institute Zed Drama Prize and AFI's Directing Workshop for Women. Alisa is also an accomplished singer/songwriter and has recorded two EPs that have received airplay on Los Angeles' Indy 103.1 and KCRW.

She is currently penning her second feature length romantic comedy.
